Namsor Pricing

  1. Home
  2. Services

With NamSor BASIC, you can process small volumes of data monthly with no charge : up to 1000 names (to infer gender) and 100 names (to infer origin).

Beyond that, the following charges will apply : $0.01 per extra genderized name, $0.05 per extra origined name. We ask to enter your CC details for identification reasons, but you will not be billed unless you use the API beyond the free quota.

We use Stripe for secure payment processing, so we never have access to your CC details. With NamSor Gender PRO, for example, you pay a monthly subscription of 9.99$ to process up to 5000 names (to infer gender).

Beyond that, the following charges will apply : $0.005 per extra genderized name. Subscriptions can be cancelled at any time. The usage over the period is calculated on a calendar month basis and invoiced on 1st of the following month.
