Services and pricing

About our service

The free BASIC subscription allows to classify up to 500 names per month (origin, ethnicity) and 5000 names per month (gender).

We use Stripe for secure payment processing, so we never have access to your CC details. Overages as calculated according to your current subscription period, billed and reset every month. Subscriptions can be cancelled at any time. With NamSor PRO, for example, you pay a monthly subscription of 19$ to process up to 10000 names (to infer gender). Beyond that, the following charges will apply : $0.003 per extra genderized name. Ask us about ENTERPRISE pricing if you plan to process more than one million names per month.

We apply a (1 / 20) ratio on duplicate names, so you can process up to twenty times the same name.

Individual researchers

Our service is designed for use by organizations (Companies and start-ups, Universities, Government bodies, NGOs, ...), not by individuals. Individual researchers and students should register as a University after getting approval from their supervisor.
